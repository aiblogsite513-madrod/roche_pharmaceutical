Roche Pharmaceutical

Medical Equipment Import & Distribution Company

Roche Pharmaceutical is a Colombo-based medical equipment import and distribution company located in Bambalapitiya, Colombo-04. We supply high-quality medical equipment and healthcare solutions to hospitals, clinics, laboratories, and healthcare institutions across Sri Lanka.Our operations are guided by ethical business practices, strict quality assurance, and a strong commitment to improving healthcare delivery through reliable medical technology.
